Inorder to protect the health of all our Volunteers and people around us we have established COVID-19 protocols that all volunteer must follow. By selecting I AGREE WITH TERMS AND CONDITIONS, you acknowledge that you will adhere to the following COVID-19 safety requirements.

  1. I work in my own region and I and my team follow all qurantain guidlines assigned by government.
  2. Not to participate in the Project if I have had a body temperature of 100.4 degrees or higher during the previous 14 days or have suffered any symptoms of the COVID-19 virus, the flu, or the common cold.
